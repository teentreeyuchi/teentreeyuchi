1. It seems that diagrams can be an attempt to arrive at the conclusion of the meaning of something. But rather than directly defining what meaning is, if that even can be defined, it carefully dissects meaning down into a few or many clues, and hopefully a comprehensive look at those individual qualities can help the questioners arrive at some sort of a conclusion. Diagrams are a thought process to attempt to understand a thought. Diagrams in this sense can help us dive in and take a closer look, but also forces us to think about “meaning” in more than just our usual assumed way.

A diagram made with the intent of art or artist’ eyes behind it perhaps as this article suggests blend the objective and the subjective qualities and create a sort of romantic objective outlook.
