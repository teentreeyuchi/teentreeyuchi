The Girl with the Green Ribbon

back

There once was a girl named Jenny.
She always wore a green ribbon around her neck.

This is Jenny's childhood friend Alfred.
Alfred fancies Jenny very much and wants to marry her.

Alfred always asks "Why do you always wear the green ribbon?"
Jenny shakes her head and says nothing.
She never tells him.

Finally, Jenny and Alfred got married.
Jenny still doesn't tell Alfred anything even though he keeps asking.
Happily married, Jenny is torn by her sworn secret.

One day, Jenny falls ill to a deadly disease.

"Now I shall unravel your secret," thinks Alfred to himself as he starts pulling on the ribbon."

In the end, Alfred pulls so hard that Jenny's head falls off.
Her secret's been revealed.
she's been a severed head this whole time.
was it worth it?
